This page will be expanded as more girls and women in science emerge!

Contemporary

Diana Cowern, science outreach coordinator at UCSD and Youtube sensation
Jane Goodall, pioneer environmental scientist
Ann Makosinski, AKA Flashlight Girl
Marissa Mayer, President & CEO of Yahoo!, formerly Google's first female engineer
Sabrina Pasterski, astrophysics grad student known for advances in high energy physics
Sara Sakowitz, 18 year old cancer researcher, founder of Build Her Future, and co-founder of Blue Moon Box

Historic

Elizabeth Blackwell, first female doctor in America
Rachel Carson, conservationist and marine biologist, author of Silent Spring
Marie Curie, the first woman ever to win the Nobel Prize (Physics 1903)
Rosalind Franklin, helped discover the structure of DNA
Hypatia, the first female mathematician according to written history
Madame Marie-Anne Lavoisier, wife and colleague of the Father of Chemistry
Barbara McClintock, discovered "jumping genes" using corn, recipient of Nobel Prize in Medicine 1983
